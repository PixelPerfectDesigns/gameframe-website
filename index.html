    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions

    What kind of photos work best?

    High-quality, well-lit photos where the athlete is clearly visible. Phone photos are perfectly fine! Action shots or posed photos work great - just avoid heavily blurred or dark images.

    How long does it take?

    Most designs are completed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive both high-resolution digital files and physical cards shipped to your door.

    Can I request changes?

    Yes! One round of revisions is included with every order. We'll work with you to make sure you love your sports card design.

    What sports do you cover?

    All sports! Baseball, basketball, football, soccer, hockey, tennis, wrestling, gymnastics, and more. Each design is customized for the specific sport and team.

    Do you offer team discounts?

    Yes! We offer special pricing for team orders of 10+ players. Contact us directly for custom team packages and bulk pricing.

    What if I'm not satisfied?

    Your satisfaction is guaranteed! We'll work with you until you love your design, or provide a full refund if we can't meet your expectations.
