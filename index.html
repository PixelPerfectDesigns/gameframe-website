FAQ

What kind of photos work best?

Bright, in focus images with the athlete centered. Avoid heavy motion blur. Phone photos are fine.

How do I print the design?

You will receive a high resolution file you can upload to any photo printer or canvas service.

Revisions

One revision is included for small tweaks like color, name, or number formatting.
